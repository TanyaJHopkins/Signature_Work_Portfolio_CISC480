Projects

Red Panel Eurorack Series (Buchla USA)

Many moons ago, I assisted with the manufacturing and QA of the Buchla Red Panel series of eurorack modules when I worked for Buchla USA.

CPU Simulator Project (CISC 340)

Elementary Japanese II Video Project (JAPN 112)

In my second year Japanese class, we had to make a video project to show our comprehension of the language. Me and my partner ended up making a weird J-Horror-inspired skit that was quite fun to make, even if it only ever got to be 60% complete.
