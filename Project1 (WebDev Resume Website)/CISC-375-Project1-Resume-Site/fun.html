Interests

Music

I've been learning about and writing music since around my freshman year of high school, and through that effort I have made some great friends and even got a job working on a podcast here at UST with those same skills. I listen to music as obsessively as I involve myself in it--though over a much greater span of time.

Art / Design

I enjoy various forms of visual and conceptual art, as well as the study of said art. Lately, I've found myself building a collection of niche design books for the sake of archiving them (and staring at the pretty pictures, of course). The background to this webpage serves a good example of my art, as I made it in Blender specifically for this project.

Technology

I have always loved technology, and will continue to love both it and the various devices I find myself surrounded with.
